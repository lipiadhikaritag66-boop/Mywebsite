Admissions 2081/82

Apply now for our undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Limited seats available.

Eligibility

Who Can Apply?

Check the eligibility criteria for each program before applying.

💻

BIM / BBA / Economics

  • ✅ Passed 10+2 or equivalent
  • ✅ Minimum 45% aggregate marks
  • ✅ Any stream acceptable
  • ✅ Must be under 25 years of age
🖥️

BSc.CSIT

  • ✅ Passed 10+2 Science
  • ✅ Must have Math as subject
  • ✅ Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • ✅ Entrance exam required
🎓

MBA

  • ✅ Bachelor's degree (any discipline)
  • ✅ Minimum 50% in graduation
  • ✅ GMAT / TU entrance exam
  • ✅ Work experience preferred
Process

How to Apply

Step 1

Fill Online Application

Complete the admission form below with accurate personal and academic details.

Step 2

Submit Documents

Submit copies of academic certificates, citizenship, and passport-size photos at the admission office.

Step 3

Entrance Exam (if applicable)

BSc.CSIT and MBA applicants must appear for the TU entrance examination.

Step 4

Merit List Publication

Selected candidates will be notified via phone/email and the merit list will be published on our website.

Step 5

Fee Payment & Enrollment

Pay the admission fee within the given deadline to confirm your enrollment. Scholarships will be applied at this stage.
